🥒 SGDC Pickle Ball mini Tourney

Info Teams Scoring Bracket Admin

Format

Mixed-skill doubles, single open division. 12 teams, 3 pools of 4, round-robin Group Stage then 6-team knockout bracket.

📋 Full tournament details (rules, schedule, prize pool, skill-tier guide) live in Notion: Pickle Ball Tourney Notion page →

Skill tiers

  • 🟢 Beginner = 1 point
  • 🟡 Intermediate = 2 points
  • 🔴 Advanced = 3 points

Team balance rule: combined points must equal 3 or 4.

Scoring

  • Group Stage: single game to 11, side-out scoring, win by 2
  • Knockout QF: single game to 11
  • Knockout SF: single game to 15
  • 3rd-place playoff: single game to 11
  • Final: best of 3 games to 11

Schedule

  • Session 1 — Tue Jul 7 — Round 1 (Magnum, 2 courts)
  • Session 2 — Fri Jul 17 — Round 2 (Magnum, 1 court)
  • Session 3 — Tue Jul 28 — Round 3 (Magnum, 2 courts)
  • Playoffs — TBD (target 1st week of August)

How to use this site

  1. Open the Teams tab and add each approved team as signups roll in.
  2. Once all 12 teams are registered, click Draw pools for a balanced snake-draft.
  3. During each session, enter scores in the Scoring tab — standings update live.
  4. After all 18 pool matches are scored, the Bracket tab unlocks with seeded playoffs.
  5. The Admin tab lets you back up or reset the tournament data.
